Query 1:
Highest Average Battery Capacity Brand

Purpose:
Identify the manufacturer with the highest average battery capacity.

Insight:
Mercedes-Benz has the highest average battery capacity among all manufacturers in the dataset.

=================================================

Query 2:
Vehicle with the Highest Battery Capacity

Purpose:
Identify the EV model with the largest battery pack.

Insight:
Mercedes-Benz EQS 450 4MATIC has the highest battery capacity in the dataset.

=================================================

Key Learnings:

1. Subqueries allow one query to use the result of another query.

2. CTEs improve readability and simplify complex SQL logic.

3. CTEs are useful for breaking large analytical queries into manageable steps.

4. Subqueries are commonly used for comparisons against aggregate values such as averages and maximums.

=================================================

Business Relevance:

CTEs and Subqueries are frequently used in:
- KPI calculations
- Dashboard development
- Customer segmentation
- Product performance analysis
- Financial reporting
- Business intelligence solutions
